product-service deployed to namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }}.

Check pod status:
  kubectl get pods -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l app=product-service

Port-forward to test locally (in a separate terminal):
  kubectl port-forward -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} svc/product-service 3003:{{ .Values.service.port }}

Then, in your original terminal:
  curl http://localhost:3003/health

Note: every replica runs its own RabbitMQ consumer bound to the same queue,
so with 2+ replicas RabbitMQ load-balances order.created events across them
automatically (the "competing consumers" pattern) — nothing extra to configure.
