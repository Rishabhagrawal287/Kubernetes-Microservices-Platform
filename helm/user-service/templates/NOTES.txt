user-service deployed to namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }}.

Check pod status:
  kubectl get pods -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l app=user-service

Port-forward to test locally (in a separate terminal):
  kubectl port-forward -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} svc/user-service 3001:{{ .Values.service.port }}

Then, in your original terminal:
  curl http://localhost:3001/health
